SEO Services

SEO is the process of optimizing your website's content, technical setup, and reach in order to increase its search engine ranking for a specific keyword. SEO is essentially a set of effective digital marketing methods. Pelikonz Innovations staff has an established track record of producing exceptional results and ensuring client satisfaction. This is accomplished by employing tactics such as adding plugins, increasing page loading speed, and building internal links. Algorithms are used by all major search engines, including Google, Yahoo, and Bing, to determine the relevancy and popularity of online pages, videos, and local listings. These parameters are used to determine the order of the material in search results. You can improve your rankings and generate natural backlinks by following best practices such as producing high-quality original content, incorporating headings for indexing purposes, using strong keywords in URL structures, and properly utilizing H tags for summaries and unique meta descriptions.

Google Paid Campaign

Google Ads is a tool that enables businesses to promote their products and services on Google's search engine results pages (SERPs). It's a pay-per-click (PPC) advertising platform that allows businesses to create ads, bid on certain keywords, and target specific demographics. Google Ads is managed online through a user-friendly interface that allows businesses to create, modify, and optimize ad campaigns to achieve their advertising goals. Businesses may use Google Ads to reach out to potential customers who are looking for similar products or services, and they only pay when someone clicks on their ad. This platform enables businesses to set an advertising budget and change it at any time based on their advertising needs.
